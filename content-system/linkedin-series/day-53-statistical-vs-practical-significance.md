# Day 53: Statistical vs Practical Significance

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 53 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Statistical Significance vs Practical Significance

Statistical significance means the result is unlikely to be due to chance.

Practical significance means the result is actually useful in real life.

Real-life example:

A company changes a button color on its website.

Conversion rate increases from 10.00% to 10.05%.

With a very large sample, this may be statistically significant.

But is it practically useful?

Maybe not.

The increase may be too small to matter in business terms.

Data analytics use:

Do not stop at p-value.

Always ask:

Is the effect large enough to matter?

Key takeaway:

A result can be statistically significant and still practically useless.
```
