# Day 13: Probability

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 13 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Probability

Probability measures the chance of an event happening.

It ranges from 0 to 1.

0 means impossible.

1 means certain.

Example:

When tossing a fair coin, the probability of getting heads is:

1/2 = 0.5

Real-life example:

A bank wants to estimate the probability that a customer will default on a loan.

A hospital wants to estimate the probability that a patient will be readmitted.

A company wants to estimate the probability that a customer will buy a product.

Key takeaway:

Probability helps us measure uncertainty.

Statistics helps us make decisions under uncertainty.
```
