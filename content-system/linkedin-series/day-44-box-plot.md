# Day 44: Box Plot

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 44 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Box Plot

A box plot is a visual summary of numerical data.

It shows:

Minimum
First quartile
Median
Third quartile
Maximum
Outliers

Real-life example:

A company compares salaries across departments using box plots.

One department may have a higher median salary.

Another may have more salary variation.

Another may have extreme salary outliers.

Data analytics use:

Box plots are useful for comparing distributions across groups.

Examples:

Marks across classes.
Salary across departments.
Sales across regions.
Delivery time across cities.

Key takeaway:

A box plot shows center, spread, and outliers in one visual.
```
