# Day 10: Range, IQR, and Outliers

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 10 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Range, IQR, and Outliers

Range tells us the difference between the maximum and minimum value.

Range = Maximum value - Minimum value

But range is affected by extreme values.

That is why we use IQR.

IQR means interquartile range.

IQR = Q3 - Q1

It measures the spread of the middle 50% of data.

Real-life example:

House prices in an area:

₹30L, ₹35L, ₹40L, ₹45L, ₹3Cr

The range becomes very large because of ₹3Cr.

But IQR gives a better idea of normal price variation.

Key takeaway:

Range gives total spread.

IQR gives reliable spread.

Outliers can distort analysis if ignored.
```
