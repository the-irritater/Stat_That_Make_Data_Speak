# Day 30: Statistics Roadmap for Data Science

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 30 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Statistics Roadmap for Data Science

If you want to learn statistics for data science, do not learn randomly.

Follow a proper roadmap.

Step 1: Basic statistics

Mean, median, mode, variance, standard deviation, skewness, kurtosis.

Step 2: Probability

Probability rules, conditional probability, Bayes’ theorem.

Step 3: Distributions

Normal, binomial, Poisson, exponential distributions.

Step 4: Sampling and estimation

Sampling methods, sampling distribution, confidence intervals.

Step 5: Hypothesis testing

p-value, Type I error, Type II error, t-test, chi-square test, ANOVA.

Step 6: Regression

Linear regression, logistic regression, assumptions, interpretation.

Step 7: Advanced topics

Time series, quality control, experimental design, A/B testing, machine learning statistics.

Real-life advice:

Do not only memorize formulas.

For every topic, learn:

What it means  
Where it is used  
How to calculate it  
How to interpret it  
How to explain it in an interview  

Key takeaway:

Statistics is not difficult when learned in the right order.

Start simple.

Stay consistent.

Connect every concept to real data.
```
