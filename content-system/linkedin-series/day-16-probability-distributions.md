## LinkedIn Post Copy

```text
Day 16 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Probability Distributions

A probability distribution shows how probabilities are spread across possible outcomes.

In simple words:

It tells us what values are possible and how likely they are.

Real-life example:

Suppose we count the number of customers visiting a store every hour.

Some hours may have 5 customers.

Some may have 20.

Some may have 50.

A probability distribution helps us understand the pattern of these possible values.

Common distributions include:

Normal distribution  
Binomial distribution  
Poisson distribution  
Exponential distribution  

Key takeaway:

Probability distribution helps us understand the behavior of random variables.
```
