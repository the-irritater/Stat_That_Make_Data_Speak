# Day 54: Effect Size

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 54 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Effect Size

Effect size tells us how large or meaningful a difference is.

A p-value tells us whether a result is statistically significant.

Effect size tells us how strong the result is.

Real-life example:

Two teaching methods are compared.

Method B gives higher marks than Method A.

The p-value says the difference is significant.

But effect size tells us whether the improvement is small, moderate, or large.

Data analytics use:

Effect size is important in research, experiments, A/B testing, psychology, education, healthcare, and business analytics.

Key takeaway:

p-value answers: Is there evidence of a difference?

Effect size answers: How much difference is there?
```
