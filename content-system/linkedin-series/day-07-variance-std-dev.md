# Day 7: Variance and Standard Deviation

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 7 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Variance and Standard Deviation

Mean tells us the center of data.

But it does not tell us how spread out the data is.

That is where variance and standard deviation help.

Variance measures how far values are from the mean.

Standard deviation shows the average spread from the mean.

Real-life example:

Two students have the same average score of 70.

Student A scores:

68, 70, 72

Student B scores:

40, 70, 100

Both have the same average.

But Student B’s marks are more spread out.

So, Student B has higher standard deviation.

Key takeaway:

Mean tells us the center.

Standard deviation tells us consistency.
```
