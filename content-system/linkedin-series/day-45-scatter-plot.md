# Day 45: Scatter Plot

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 45 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Scatter Plot

A scatter plot shows the relationship between two numerical variables.

Each point represents one observation.

Real-life example:

A student dataset may compare:

Study hours and exam marks.

If points move upward from left to right, there may be a positive relationship.

More study hours may be associated with higher marks.

Another example:

Screen time and sleep duration.

If points move downward, there may be a negative relationship.

Data analytics use:

Scatter plots are useful before correlation, regression, and predictive modeling.

Key takeaway:

A scatter plot helps us see relationships before calculating them.
```
