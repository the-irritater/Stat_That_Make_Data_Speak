# Day 26: Chi-Square Test

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 26 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Chi-Square Test

A chi-square test is used for categorical data.

It helps us check whether two categorical variables are associated.

Real-life example:

A researcher wants to know whether gender and preference for online shopping are related.

Variables:

Gender: Male/Female/Other  
Shopping preference: Online/Offline  

Both are categorical.

The chi-square test helps check whether shopping preference differs by gender.

Another example:

A company may test whether product preference differs across age groups.

Key takeaway:

Chi-square test is useful when we want to study relationships between categories.
```
