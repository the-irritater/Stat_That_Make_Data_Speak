# Day 41: Frequency Table

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 41 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Frequency Table

A frequency table shows how often each category or value appears in the data.

Real-life example:

Suppose a survey asks students their preferred learning mode.

Online: 120
Offline: 90
Hybrid: 140

This frequency table quickly shows that hybrid learning is the most preferred option.

Frequency tables are useful for categorical data.

Examples:

Gender
Department
City
Product category
Payment mode
Customer segment

Data analytics use:

Frequency tables help summarize survey responses, customer groups, product choices, and categorical variables.

Key takeaway:

A frequency table is one of the simplest ways to make categorical data readable.
```
