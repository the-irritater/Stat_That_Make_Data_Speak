# Day 40: Exploratory Data Analysis

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 40 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Exploratory Data Analysis

Exploratory Data Analysis, or EDA, means understanding the data before applying models or tests.

EDA helps answer:

What variables are present?
Are there missing values?
Are there outliers?
What patterns exist?
Which variables are related?
Is the data balanced or skewed?

Real-life example:

Before predicting house prices, we first explore area, location, price distribution, number of rooms, and outliers.

Without EDA, we may build a model without understanding the data.

Data analytics use:

EDA is used in almost every data analytics, statistics, machine learning, and research project.

Key takeaway:

Do not jump directly to modeling.

First understand the data.
```
