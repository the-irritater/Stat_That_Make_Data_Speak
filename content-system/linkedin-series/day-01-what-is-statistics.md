# Day 1: What is Statistics?

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 1 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: What is Statistics?

Statistics is the science of collecting, organizing, analyzing, interpreting, and presenting data.

In simple words:

Statistics helps us understand data and make better decisions.

Real-life example:

Suppose a college wants to know whether students are satisfied with online lectures.

They collect feedback from 500 students.

But raw responses alone are not useful.

Statistics helps summarize those responses, calculate percentages, identify patterns, and support decisions.

For example:

If 72% of students say online lectures are useful, the college may continue hybrid learning.

Key takeaway:

Statistics converts raw data into meaningful decisions.

Without statistics, data is just numbers.

With statistics, data starts speaking.
```
