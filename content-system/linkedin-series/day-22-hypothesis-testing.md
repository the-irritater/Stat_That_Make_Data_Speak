# Day 22: Hypothesis Testing

## LinkedIn Post Copy
```text
Day 22 of Stats That Make Data Speak

Topic: Hypothesis Testing

Hypothesis testing is a statistical method used to make decisions using sample data.

It starts with two statements:

Null hypothesis and alternative hypothesis.

Null hypothesis usually says there is no effect or no difference.

Alternative hypothesis says there is an effect or difference.

Real-life example:

A company wants to know whether a new website design increases conversion rate.

Null hypothesis:

The new design does not improve conversion rate.

Alternative hypothesis:

The new design improves conversion rate.

The company collects data and performs a statistical test.

Based on the result, it decides whether the observed difference is likely real or just due to chance.

Key takeaway:

Hypothesis testing helps us make evidence-based decisions.
```
