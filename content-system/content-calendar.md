# Content Calendar — Stats That Make Data Speak

## Posting Strategy

Do **not** post all 30 days at once.

Best frequency: **4 posts per week** is better than daily posting if daily posting reduces quality.

Start with the announcement post first. Then post Day 1 after 1 day. Then continue the series consistently.

## Daily Engagement (10 min/day)

- Comment on 10 data/analytics posts on LinkedIn
- Respond to all comments on your own posts
- Connect with people who engage with your content

---

## Batching Strategy

Spend **60–90 minutes once per week** creating all posts:

1. **Sunday evening:** Prepare the next 4–5 post drafts using templates
2. **Schedule:** Use LinkedIn's native scheduler or Buffer
3. **Daily (10 min):** Engage with comments and other posts
4. **Monthly:** Review analytics, double down on what worked
