# Template: Carousel Post Guide

Carousels get 2–3× more engagement than text posts. Use them for concept breakdowns.

---

## Technical Specs

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Dimensions** | 1080 × 1350 px (portrait) |
| **Format** | PDF upload |
| **Slides** | 5–7 (sweet spot) |
| **Font size** | 24px minimum (mobile readability) |
| **Tools** | Canva (free) or Adobe Express |

---

## Slide Structure

### Slide 1: Hook
- Bold, benefit-driven headline
- No subtitle needed — the headline does all the work
- Your branding (name + tagline) at bottom

**Examples:**
- "5 Statistical Concepts Every Data Analyst Must Know"
- "Stop Misusing P-Values. Here's the Correct Way."
- "Mean vs Median: When Each One Lies to You"

### Slides 2–5: Content (1 concept per slide)
- **One idea per slide** — if you need two, make two slides
- Use visuals: charts, icons, diagrams
- Large text, minimal words
- High contrast colors

### Slide 6: Summary / Key Takeaway
- Recap the main points
- One bold takeaway line

### Slide 7: CTA + Branding
- "Follow for more data insights"
- "Full details + Python code: [GitHub repo]"
- Your name + LinkedIn handle
- "Stats That Make Data Speak" brand

---

## Design Tips

1. **Use consistent colors** — pick 2–3 brand colors and stick to them
2. **White space is your friend** — don't cram slides
3. **Charts > text** — if you can show it visually, do
4. **Number your slides** — "3/7" helps people track progress
5. **Test on mobile** — 80% of LinkedIn is mobile, make sure text is readable

---

## Content Formula

```
1 concept → 1 visual → 1 insight
```

**Concept:** The statistical idea you're teaching
**Visual:** A chart, diagram, or comparison that makes it click
**Insight:** Why this matters in the real world

---

## Example Carousel Outline

**Title:** "Correlation vs Causation — The #1 Mistake in Data"

| Slide | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | "Correlation ≠ Causation. But what DOES it mean?" |
| 2 | Definition: What correlation actually measures |
| 3 | Visual: Ice cream sales vs drowning (classic example) |
| 4 | The hidden variable: temperature |
| 5 | Real business example: Ad spend vs revenue |
| 6 | How to test for causation (experiments, controls) |
| 7 | CTA: "Follow + check my GitHub for Python examples" |

---

## Workflow

1. Write the content outline (use this template)
2. Open Canva → search "LinkedIn Carousel"
3. Pick a template matching your brand colors
4. Fill in slides following the structure above
5. Export as PDF
6. Upload to LinkedIn as a document post
7. Write a 2-line caption with the hook
