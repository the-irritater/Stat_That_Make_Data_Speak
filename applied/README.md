#  Applied Edition — Stats With Python & Real Data

> *Theory means nothing without application. Every notebook here answers a real business question.*

## The Story Arc

These 7 notebooks follow a **progression** — each builds on the previous:

| # | Notebook | Purpose | Business Question |
|---|----------|---------|-------------------|
| 1 | [What does our sales data actually look like?](notebooks/01-what-does-sales-data-look-like.ipynb) | **Understand data** | What are we working with? |
| 2 | [Analyzing customer spending patterns](notebooks/02-customer-spending-patterns.ipynb) | **Summarize behavior** | Where does the money come from? |
| 3 | [What actually drives sales?](notebooks/03-what-drives-sales.ipynb) | **Find relationships** | Which factors matter most? |
| 4 | [Predicting customer spend](notebooks/04-predicting-customer-spend.ipynb) | **Build prediction** | Can we forecast revenue? |
| 5 | [Do discounts increase repeat purchases?](notebooks/05-do-discounts-work.ipynb) | **Test a decision** | Should we keep running promos? |
| 6 | [Who are our best buyers?](notebooks/06-who-are-best-buyers.ipynb) | **Segment users** | How do we target the right people? |
| 7 | [Is this campaign actually working?](notebooks/07-is-campaign-working.ipynb) | **Validate impact** | Are we wasting marketing budget? |

## Every Notebook Produces
