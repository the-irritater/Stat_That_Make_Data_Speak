# Monetization Path

Don't rush this. Build trust first, monetize later.

---

## Timeline

```
Month 1–2: BUILD TRUST (free value only)
    ↓
Month 3: TEST (small paid asset)
    ↓
Month 4+: SCALE (expand paid offerings)
```

---

## Phase 1: Free Tier (Month 1–2)

Everything is free. Your job is to build audience + credibility.

| Asset | Platform | Purpose |
|-------|----------|---------|
| LinkedIn posts | LinkedIn | Visibility + authority |
| GitHub repo | GitHub | Proof of work + discoverability |
| Module content | GitHub | Educational value |
| Notebooks | GitHub | Technical credibility |

**Success metrics before moving to Phase 2:**
- [ ] 500+ LinkedIn followers in your niche
- [ ] 50+ GitHub stars
- [ ] Consistent posting for 8+ weeks
- [ ] At least 2 posts with 50+ likes
- [ ] DMs/comments asking "do you have a course?"

> Don't move to Phase 2 until you see organic demand. If nobody's asking, you're not ready.

---

## Phase 2: Test (Month 3)

Launch ONE small paid asset to test demand.

### Option A: PDF Guide — "Stats That Make Data Speak"
- **Price:** $9–$19
- **Platform:** Gumroad or Lemon Squeezy
- **Content:** Curated version of your best 15 posts + 5 applied examples + 3 case studies
- **Format:** Clean PDF, 40–60 pages
- **Positioning:** "The statistics guide I wish existed when I started in data analytics"

### Option B: Notion Template — "Data Analysis Playbook"
- **Price:** $5–$12
- **Platform:** Gumroad
- **Content:** Analysis workflow templates + checklists + dataset recommendations
- **Positioning:** "My exact process for going from messy data to business insights"

### How to launch:
1. Tease it in 2–3 posts: "I'm putting together something…"
2. Post a launch announcement with the first chapter free
3. Link in bio + pinned post
4. Offer free copies to 5 people who engaged most (creates reviews)

---

## Phase 3: Scale (Month 4+)

Based on what works in Phase 2, expand:

| Offering | Price | When |
|----------|-------|------|
| Updated PDF guide (v2) | $19–$29 | Month 4 |
| Mini course (video) | $29–$49 | Month 5–6 |
| 1:1 mentorship for beginners | $50–$100/hr | When demand exists |
| LinkedIn Newsletter (premium) | Free (ad-supported) | Month 4 |

---

## Rules

1. **Never paywall your GitHub repo** — it's your growth engine
2. **Free content = top of funnel** → Paid = structured + curated
3. **Paid content must be 10× better** than free, not just reformatted
4. **Start with the cheapest product** to validate demand before building expensive ones
5. **Price based on value, not time** — "save 20 hours of confused Googling" > "60 pages of content"

---

## Revenue Targets (realistic)

| Month | Expected Revenue | Source |
|-------|-----------------|--------|
| 1–2 | $0 | Building trust |
| 3 | $50–$200 | PDF guide (10–20 sales) |
| 4–6 | $200–$500/month | Guide + mentorship |
| 6–12 | $500–$2,000/month | Course + guide + mentorship |

These are conservative estimates. The real value is in the opportunities (jobs, freelance, speaking) that come from the authority you build.
